Understanding Polarity and Subjectivity

Polarity and subjectivity are key concepts in sentiment analysis:

Polarity

  • What It Is: Measures the positivity or negativity of text.
  • Scale: Ranges from -1 (extremely negative) to +1 (extremely positive).
  • Interpretation: High positive scores indicate positive sentiment; high negative scores indicate negative sentiment. Scores near 0 are neutral.

Subjectivity

  • What It Is: Indicates how much personal opinion or emotion is in the text.
  • Scale: Ranges from 0 (very objective) to 1 (very subjective).
  • Interpretation: Higher scores mean more personal opinion and emotion; lower scores suggest factual, objective content.
